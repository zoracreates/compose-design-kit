Buttons

Compose comes with a variety of styled buttons. To use a Compose button, add one of the cdk-btn or cdk-btn-inverted classes to an <a> or a <button> element. You will notice that all cdk-btn-inverted buttons have white text, a white border, and a transparent background. However, their hover style changes depending on the suffix used.

cdk-btn Classes

<button class="cdk-btn-default">Default</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-primary">Primary</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-dark">Dark</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-danger">Danger</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-deactivated">Deactivated</button>

cdk-btn-inverted Classes

<button class="cdk-btn-inverted-default">Default</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-inverted-primary">Primary</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-inverted-dark">Dark</button>
