Buttons

Compose comes with a variety of styled buttons. To use a Compose button, add one of the cdk-btn classes to an <a> or a <button> element. 

<button class="cdk-btn-default">Default</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-primary">Primary</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-dark">Dark</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-danger">Danger</button>
<button class="cdk-btn-deactivated">Deactivated</button>
