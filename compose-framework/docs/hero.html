Hero

The Compose hero component centers its content vertically and horizontally, and takes up 100% of the width of its container. It also takes a minimum height equal to 50% of the window's height. To use it, simply add a class of cdk-hero. 

<div class="cdk-hero">
    <div class="cdk-wrapper">
        <h2>Hero Preview</h2>
        <p>Hello, I am a hero example.</p>
    </div>
</div>
